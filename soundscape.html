NYC Soundscape Visualization | 纽约声景研究
Finding out the Audio Identity of A Place

采集不同地点的声音名片

Soundscape is the sound or the combination of sounds that forms an immersive environment. It is tightly connected with the cultural and social environment.

I visualized the soundscape in different places for half an hour each place. By the means of speaking to the visual sense, this work aims at making people aware of the sound environment in which they are immersed in every moment and arising the sharpness of the aural sense in this age of "visual first" culture.

声景是一个特点环境的声音集合。它与文化和社会环境有着紧密联系。本人在纽约市的数个不同地点进行各长达30分钟的声音采集并实时视觉化成图形。这样做的目的是希望在视觉优先的文化环境中，通过诉诸惯用的视觉感官，唤起人们对随时浸没其中的声音环境的感知，和听觉感官的敏锐度。
