About Me

I’m a new media artist and interaction designer. My passion lies in the intersection of art, design and tech, and how information and story can be communicated through an engaging experience.

- was showcased in 12th International Conference on Advances in Computer Entertainment Technology (ACE International Conference) in November 16-19th, 2015 in Malaysia. The related design paper was included in ACM(Association for Computing Machinery) Digital Library.

- documentation video demoed in CoInvent Pulse 2015 Tech Festival in New York

- documentation video demoed in Feltrinelli Foundation in Milan, Italy

个人介绍

我是一个新媒体艺术创作者和交互设计师。我对艺术、设计和科技的交叉领域充满兴趣。我关注信息与叙事如何通过吸引人的互动体验来得以传达。
